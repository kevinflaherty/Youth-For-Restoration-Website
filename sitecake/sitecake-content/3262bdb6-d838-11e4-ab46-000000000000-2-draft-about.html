About Us

Founded in 2012, Youth for Restoration (YFR) is a nonprofit, tax-exempt charitable organization under Section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code. The mission of the organization is to engage local communities to identify and restore old historic sites and pursue their nomination to the State and National Register of Historic Places.
